Department of Physical Education

At BANSALS, the endeavor of the Department of Physical Education is to achieve excellence in sports. Participation in different sports fosters leadership, teamwork, discipline, and confidence in the students. The focus of the Department is on gender equity and diversity in sports, to ensure a friendly and fearless environment and to instill the values of fair play and sportsmanship in students.

The department offers opportunities and facilitates the participation of students in sports tournaments and championships at College and University level in numerous games like Aquatics, Archery, Athletics, Badminton, Ball Badminton, Baseball, Basketball, Cross Country, Chess, Cricket, Football, Hockey, Handball, Judo, Korfball, Netball, Softball, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, Taekwondo, Yoga, etc.

XYZ students have shown their mettle in games, like Badminton, Table Tennis, Basketball, Swimming, Chess, Tennis, Softball and Baseball, and bagged medals at national and international tournaments and championships.

Objectives of Department of Physical Education

To provide quality sports facilities allowing students to excel in sports.
facilitate students to maximize their sporting potential and find a game that they can participate and enjoy.
To develop sense of fair play, honest competition and sportsmanship.To promote physical and mental well-being of students.
To develop self-esteem and self-confidence in students through positive sporting experiences.
To develop leadership skills and foster qualities of co-operation, tolerance, consideration, trust and responsibility through sporting activities and events.
To provide a release of students in order to refresh them for academic work.
To develop individual and teams in games with high skills so as to achieve excellence.

Sports Infrastructures and Facilities

The Hostel has impressive state-of-the-art sports infrastructure comprising of multipurpose sports indoor stadium and a vast lush green sports field.

Multipurpose Sports Stadium

The multipurpose sports stadium is centrally air-conditioned to provide high level of comfort to the players and spectators. The central court is made of high-class maple-wood and designed for games like Badminton, Basket Ball, Wrestling and Judo amongst others.The multipurpose stadium also has rooms designated for sports such as Table Tennis.

The seating for 1200 persons is evenly distributed and designed for best viewing of the event from any position. For the players, spacious changing rooms, sauna bath and showers have been provided with hygienic public conveniences. Multi-gyms, sports injury & rehabilitation center and medical room are the supporting feature of the infrastructure.

The multipurpose stadium also houses the fitness center and physiotherapy center. Additionally, the multipurpose stadium also houses the Basic Health Care Unit of the Hostel.

Fitness Center

The Fitness Center is located onthe top floor multipurpose hall in the sports complex. The Center cater to Free Weights Exercises, Bodyweight Exercises, Gym Ball Exercises /Swiss Ball Exercises, Resistance Band Exercises, Resistance Machine Exercises and Stretching exercises, and also hold classes for kickboxing, yoga and aerobics are some of the facilities available at the center.

The Fitness Center has two separate sections:

Weight training section: Treadmills, elliptical trainer, cycles etc.Cardio section: Olympic weight lifting weights, exercises stations, Swiss balls etc

Physiotherapy Center

The Department provides Physiotherapy services to staff and students along with other basic medical facilities.

The Physiotherapy section has an Electrotherapy room and an Exercise therapy room. Electrotherapy Room is equipped with all the basic physiotherapy modalities (i.e. IFT u.s. TENS, Muscle Stimulator, SWD [short move diathermy], Traction Machine, wax bath therapy and hydro collator) meant for pain relief and relaxation.

Exercise Room has the basic exercise equipment (i.e. Shoulder Wheel, Wrist Exerciser, Ankle Exerciser, and Foot Exerciser) along with resistance band loops, Swiss Balls, Foam Rollers, Base Balls, Medicine Balls, Weigh Cuffs and newly placed RIP-60 trainer (suspension trainer) for rehabilitation and training purposes for student sports players.
